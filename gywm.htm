关于我们


　　创始于1999年的凯萨琳娜建筑设计咨询有限公司，一直致力于新型节能的国际化房屋设计理念。从最适宜于人类居住的加拿大国带回的房屋养生理念和技术，已向中国乃至国际市场提供诸多多元化的环保室内外设计理念。我们的中外专家团将竭尽全力为您打造让您和客户满意的未来之屋。


　　Founded in 1999, Casa-Lina design Inc. has been committed to the new energy-saving international housing design. By Importing the concept from Canada, one of the world’s most livable countries, we combine the elements between health and technology. Casa-Lina has been the first mover to provide a wide range of environmental protection indoor and outdoor design concept to both domestic and international market. Our team of Chinese and foreign experts will make every effort to create for you and your customers satisfied with the future of the House.
